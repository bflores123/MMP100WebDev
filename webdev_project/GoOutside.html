Zoey's Adventure

"Girls On The Run 5K - November 11, 2012 - 14"by Old Creeper is licensed under CC BY-NC-ND 2.0

Zoey grabs onto the doorknob and takes a deep breath as she then yanks open the door. She looks around her neighborhood and notices how quiet it is. Zoey isn't sure where to go so she immediately starts running over to her neighbors house. She runs up to the door and begins banging on the door.

She screams out for help and keeps hitting the door hoping for an answer
